Space Escape

Facts about Mars

Mars is rocky with canyons, volcanoes and craters all over it.
Red dust covers almost all of Mars. It has clouds and wind, just as Earth does.
Sometimes the wind blows the red dust into a dust storm.
Tiny dust storms can look like tornados, and large ones can cover the whole planet!
